# Anomalous Electromagnetic Moments of the Tau Lepton

This repository collects analysis tools, input files, and plotting scripts for studies of the anomalous electromagnetic moments of the tau lepton in photon-induced tau-pair production ($\gamma\gamma \to \tau^+\tau^-$) at the future lepton-hadron colliders (LHeC and LHmuC). 
It is organized as a research workspace for generating, comparing, and visualizing Standard Model and beyond-the-Standard-Model benchmark predictions, with a particular focus on collider studies at the LHeC and LHmuC. 


The goal of this repository is to provide a practical framework for:
- connecting anomalous tau-moment benchmarks to EFT/SMEFT model inputs,
- analyzing generated event samples,
- producing kinematic distributions and SM/BSM comparisons,
- and supporting phenomenological studies of tau electromagnetic couplings at
- present and future lepton-hadron colliders.
